• Market Trend

    The number-one cited barrier to homeownership is saving funds for a down payment - only 12-13% of renters believe home would be possible with a down payment of 5% or less.



    Who Home Possible Serves

    The national mean down payment in 2017 was 5%. Home Possible offers a 3% down payment solution with flexibility for a range of borrower needs.



  • Market Trend

    About 21 million millennials in the 31 largest states are mortgage ready but still rent.



    Who Home Possible Serves

    The share of Home Possible loans for millennials increased 42%, from 12.5% in 2017 to 17.7% in 2018.


  • Market Trend

    With rising home prices and interest rates, plus tight lending standards, the path to homeownership is challenging, especially for low-to-median-income borrowers and first-time homebuyers.



    Who Home Possible Serves

    First-time homebuyers make up the largest share of Home Possible borrowers at 79.3%, much higher than the 42.4% in our business overall.



  • Market Trend

    Hispanics are expected to make up the largest segment of new homebuyers by 2020.



    Who Home Possible Serves

    An average of 13.6% of Home Possible mortgages have gone to Hispanic buyers, getting us one step closer to reducing the Hispanic homeownership gap.
