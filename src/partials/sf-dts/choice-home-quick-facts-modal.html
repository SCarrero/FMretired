CHOICEHome Quick Facts

CHOICEHomeSM conventional financing for manufactured housing to help increase the availability of the quality affordable homes that borrowers want, while providing lenders with the innovative financing options they need. A CHOICEHome:


Will be appraised using the Manufactured Home Appraisal Report (Form 70B) and allow the use of site-built homes as comparable sales when there are no CHOICEHome sales available as comparables.

Please note: We're working with the Appraisal Institute to develop training to educate appraisers and appraisal management companies about factory-built housing, so they have an understanding of the CHOICEHome offering.

Will be financed using most of our conventional loan products, including construction conversion mortgages, and as little as 3 percent minimum down payment for Home Possible® borrowers and HomeOneSM first-time homebuyers.

Must meet HUD requirements for home construction, installation and setup to meet CHOICEHome eligibility. Lenders must still follow local and state laws for titling manufactured homes as real estate.

Have a CHOICEHome label affixed that

  • Certifies the home meets the CHOICEHome manufacturing eligibility requirements.
  • Is an easy and effective way for appraisers and lenders to accurately identify it as a CHOICEHome.
